Kazi Nazrul Islam

The National Poet of Bangladesh

Rebel Poet

Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of Bangladesh who is especially recognized as a Bidrohi Kobi (rebel poet) around the country. Nazrul’s famous works dealt with the revolution against the British government and other important social issues.

He opposed oppression and became a voice against fascism and fundamentalism, creating a large following in the Indian Subcontinent.

"Love has no meaning or amount." - Kazi Nazrul Islam

Awards

National Poet

Certified as the national poet of Bangladesh.
